About Manuel

Manuel Calderon is a Singer-Songwriter Guitarist from Philadelphia, PA. Finding a passion for music at a young age, he learned to play the guitar and write music like the artists that inspired him. He is currently pursuing a degree at Berklee College of Music in Boston, MA.

"All I want to do is be a light in the dark through my music, pointing people to Jesus, and inspire others just as my favorite music has influenced me."
